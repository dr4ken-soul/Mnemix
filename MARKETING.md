## Post 1 — Announcement

mnemix is a shell memory daemon — hooks into your terminal and remembers every fix you've ever run

when a command fails it searches your own history and surfaces the fix inline

no chat, no cloud, no setup beyond two commands

your terminal already has the answer

## Post 2 — Demo / Story

you know that feeling when you google an error and the top result is a stack overflow thread you answered two years ago

that's the problem mnemix fixes

it watches your terminal silently, stores every fix you figure out, and the next time that error comes up it just tells you what worked last time

built on supermemory local so everything stays on your machine

no account, no api key, nothing leaves your laptop
